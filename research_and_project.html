Research and Project


Performance Comparison of Flat Plate Collector, Evacuated Tube Collector and Evacuated Flat Plate Collector for Solar Industrial Process Heat (Ongoing)

 Performance of FPC, ETC, and EFPC will be compared to find most suitable one based on application and required temperature
 Effect of solar radiation on each collector will be investigated
 Energy and exergy efficiency of the collectors at different conditions will be evaluated

Numerical Modelling and Performance Evaluation of Evacuated Flat Plate Collector for Solar Industrial Process Heat

 Effectiveness of EFPC for industrial process heat is explored via MATLAB code
 Effect of flow rate and inlet temperature on the performance of EFPC is studied
 Economic and environmental analysis is done using operational industrial data
 Optimization of key design parameters is studied for better thermal performance

Techno-economic and environmental feasibility analysis of solar water heating system for a textile industry in Bangladesh

 Potential of solar water heating in the industries of Bangladesh was analyzed
 Numerical analysis was done from thermal, economic, and environmental perspectives
 Useful heat gain, payback period, and reduction of carbon dioxide emission was calculated
 A case study is presented for a textile industry in Bangladesh

Thermodynamic analysis of ejector enhanced Organic Rankine Cycle (EORC) for low- grade waste heat recovery

 Working principle of ejector was studied
 The effect of incorporating ejector to a simple ORC was studied
 The performance of EORC was analyzed using Python and was compared with simple ORC

Potential of waste heat recovery in different processes of the steel industry and available technologies

 Steel-making processes with relevant steps and associated temperature were studied
 Available energy recovery, re-use and recycle technologies and their potential were reviewed

Design and thermodynamic analysis of a thermal power plant

 A steam power plant based on reheat Rankine cycle with power output of 130 MW was designed stating all the assumptions and providing associated schematic and T-S diagram of the cycle
 The system was analyzed from the perspective of both 1st and 2nd laws of thermodynamics

Study on heat and mass transfer in compact heat exchanger

 A technical paper was written on compact heat exchanger
 Heat transfer surfaces and enhancement, techniques to enhance heat transfer and comparison between them, effect of fin spacing, influence of the number of tube rows and their pattern, and impact of different waffle height and fin thickness was presented from available literature

Automatically stabilized flex sensor at a fixed angle using DC motor

 Worked as the team leader and developed a system where a flex sensor was stabilized automatically to a preset angle (set as input) varying the speed of a DC motor powered fan
 LabVIEW was used to develop the system

Design and fabrication of Line Follower Robot without microcontroller

 Worked as the team leader and built a LFR using ICs only (without any microcontroller)
 Participated in National Robotech Festival 2017 and was awarded champion of the Battle of Speed segment

Design and fabrication of a Soccer playing robot

 A remote-controlled robot was built
 Participated in the segment of Soccer Bot of Mecceleration 2018 (Departmental festival of IUT)

Design and fabrication of a Line Follower Robot with microcontroller

 In this project, the line follower robot was built with microprocessor (Arduino)
